Welcome to KidsPlanet Day Care & Learning Center

Prepare your children for their brightest future. KidsPlanet Day Care & Learning Center is a educational friendly place where children are happily learning while playing! Our programs are engaging and responsive for every student. We strive to make learning fun by helping students accomplish challenges and exceed their goals, in a caring and supportive environment. We offer a fun and educational experience, designed to enhance intellectual growth and promote participatory engagement.
